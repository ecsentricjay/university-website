Admission Requirements

Undergraduate

  • 5 O'Level credits (including English & Mathematics)
  • Minimum JAMB score of 200
  • Post-UTME screening examination
  • Age requirement: Not less than 16 years

Postgraduate

  • First degree with minimum 2:1 classification
  • NYSC discharge certificate or exemption
  • Official academic transcripts
  • Two academic references

International Students

  • Equivalent qualifications to Nigerian standards
  • English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Valid student visa
  • Medical certificate and insurance
